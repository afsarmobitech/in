Welcome to Afsar Mobitech

Afsar Mobitech is a premium mobile and digital service center located in the heart of Vadodara, Gujarat. We have been serving the community with excellence and dedication, providing comprehensive solutions for all mobile and digital needs.

Our journey began with a vision to create a one-stop solution for mobile enthusiasts and digital service seekers. Today, we proudly serve thousands of satisfied customers with our wide range of services including mobile sales, accessories, expert repairs, and comprehensive CSC digital services.

At Afsar Mobitech, we believe in quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction. Our team of experienced professionals is committed to providing the best service experience with genuine products and expert solutions.

Owner: Afsarali Khan

With years of experience in the mobile and digital services industry, Afsarali Khan leads our team with expertise and dedication to customer satisfaction.
