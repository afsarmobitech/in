Our Story

Built on Trust,
Grown by Service

Afsar Mobitech began with a clear purpose: give the people of Vadodara a place they could actually trust. Honest pricing, expert hands, and no runaround. We started as a focused mobile service centre in Por GIDC and grew entirely through results and word of mouth.

Today we operate as a complete mobile and digital ecosystem — mobile sales, accessories, expert IC-level repairs, and a registered CSC centre for government digital services including Aadhaar, PAN, eKYC, banking, and documentation.

"Quality, transparency, and fair pricing have been our constants. Every device we repair and every service we deliver carries that commitment forward."

What began as one man's vision is now a recognized name in the Por GIDC community — a place where people come not just for service, but because they know they'll be treated right.

Afsarali Khan

Founder & Owner

With years of hands-on experience across mobile retail and government digital services, Afsarali Khan leads Afsar Mobitech with a direct focus on customer satisfaction and community trust.
